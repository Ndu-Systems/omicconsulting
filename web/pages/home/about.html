Company History

Makhawini Recruitment is 100% black owned and managed organaization.

Makhawini is headed up by a highly experienced young man who is passionate about the clients he serves.Makhawini is focused on providing unique, Recruitement and human resource solutions This will be achieved by understanding the needs of our clients and working together with them to produce desired results.

Values

At Makhawini Recruitment we promise to:

Develop sustainable relationships with our clients

Understand the unique needs of our clients and to provide them with customized solutions through updated market practices in order to grow their businesses whilst building and retaining a pool of talented staff

Maintain an unwavering approach and attitude to servicing our clients and exceed their expectations

Provide the perfect match and solution for the human resource needs of our clients which will be informed by our experience, insight and teamwork developed through our long-term relationships with our clients.

Vision

At the forefront of the Makhawini Trading Enterprise vision is the need to have respect for the future,culture and laws of South Africa.

The fundimental belief of Makhawini Recruitment is that long-term business relationships, and therefore mutual trust ,dignity and respect are fundamental to the success of the Recruitment partnership and progress.

To become the preferred total Recruitment solutions service provider in South Africa.

Mission

To provide integrated people to meet our clients' unique needs in a manner that reflects the highest level of integrity expected and meets the required standards expected
